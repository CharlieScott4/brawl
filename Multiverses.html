Multiversus

Releasing with an open beta in July this platofrm brawler is being developed by Warner Brothers brining characters such as Bugs Bunny, Shaggy, Batman, and Steven Universe! There are a limited amount of maps but with plans to continue this as a live service game more content is expected after launch. This game will also be free for everyone with purchasable cosmetic items such as battlepasses. The combat focuses on teamwork with every character having their own move to help their team mates. Characters also have classes such as assassian, heavy, and support each focusing on a different style of fighting. The game is fun and smooth from the small amount of time I have played so this is worth looking out for this July!
