Nickelodeon All Star Brawl

Nickelodeon All Star Brawl is a platform brawler containing many of the most popular Nickelodeon cartoon characters! This includes Spongebob, Lincoln Loud, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Garfield, and even Nigel Thornberry. This game contains 23 characters and several famous landmarks from each show to throw down. The game plays great and has very smooth combat focusing on faster movement and heavier attacks. This is definetely a game your gonna wanna check out!
