Brawlhalla

Brawlhalla is another competitive platform brawler thats free to play on all platforms. Brawlhalla takes a different character approach by using weapons such as swords, spears, bows, and more. Each character holds two of these weapons so the characters are different based on the weapons they hold. You spawn in with just your fists and then items spawn in which you compete to pick up and fight with(Your Weapons). The characters all have many purchasable skins including collabs with many major ips such as TMNT, The Walking Dead, Steven Universe, Shovel Knight, and a few more! This game is free and definetly fun although hard to learn at first.
