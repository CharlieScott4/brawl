Super Smash Bros Ultimate

Super Smash Bros is by far the most popular platform brawler franchise bringing all the nintendo characters into the ring. Many games look to this game for inspiration and a backbone for their new spin-offs. To this day this is considered the best platformer of all time with tons of maps, 82 unique characters, and even a storymode! The gameplay is fun and well pace with every character feeling completely unique. Unfortunately Smash Bros may only be played on the Nintendo Switch but if you already own won this is definetely worth the price.
